Ok...so let me preface this by saying I *might* have gone a little overboard!

That said, I numbered the skins so you can pick and choose since I'm sure adding all of them is a bit *cough* much! I think you will agree that some stand out and some are meh. Unleash the painter!  Even if none are used this was fun doing something different!   It's days like this I wish I had the time/patience to pursue 3D modeling.

These are possible VFX skins I have done that are available to the community.
