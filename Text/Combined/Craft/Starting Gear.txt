All characters start with:

Backpack of Exploration (cloak, +20 Bonus HP)
Stack of 10 Heal Kits +1
Stack of 5 Potions of Cure Light Wounds
Widget that allows you to destroy items for their gold piece value (as well as items with no gold value).

Alchemist, Rangers, Paladins, Monk, and Clerics start with
Wand of Cure Light Wounds (50 charges)

Wizards and Sorcerers start with
Staff of Magic Missile (50 charges) - Bonus Feat (random reserve feat)

Bards and Rogues start with
Stack of 5 Shield scrolls

Fighters, Barbarians, Swashbucklers, Skalds, and Scouts start with
Double the number of potions.
