Protection of Winter: At 1st level, the frost disciple gains +1 sacred bonus on all saving throws and a +1 bonus to dodge armor class.

Powerful Grip: At 3rd level, the frost disciple gains a damage bonus equal to half his Strength bonus when he attacks with a greataxe.

Frost Greataxe: At 4th level, the frost disciple can cause a greataxe he wields to become sheated in a layer of intense cold, grant the axe the
frost special ability (+1d6 cold damage). It lasts for a number of rounds equal to his class level and can be used at will.

Agonizing Strike: At 5th level, the frost disciple gains the ability to focus all the anger and hatred in his frozen heart into a single blow
once per day. He makes a normal melee attack; if he hits, he deals +1d6 points of cold damage for every 2 class levels (+2d6 at 5th, +3d6 at
6th, +4d6 at 8th, +5d6 at 10th. At 9th level, the frost disciple can use the ability twice per day.

Icy Greataxe: At 8th level, the frost disciple can cause a greataxe he wields to become sheated in a layer of intense cold, grant the axe the
icy burst special ability (+1d6 cold damage, +2d10 massive criticals). It lasts for a number of rounds equal to his class level and can be used
at will.
